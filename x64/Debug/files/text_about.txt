Help for the game "Rolling Balls"
Welcome to the computer game "Rolling Balls".
This game is designed for 2 players hitting
a white ball in turn, the winner is the player who scored the most
balls. If a player scores a white ball, the game
is considered over and that player loses.
The further away the cursor is from the white ball, the
stronger the impact will be, but remember that
it is accuracy that will bring you victory.
To close this window and go to menu press Esc.
